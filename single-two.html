Brand

Rarely has a slogan rung more true than Patek Philippe’s trademark ad line, “You never actually own a Patek Philippe. You merely look after it for the next generation.” Since 1839 the company has carried on the vision of its founders Norbert de Patek and Adrien Philippe, bringing to life countless inventions and innovations.

Owned and operated by the Stern family since 1932, Geneva’s last family-owned independent watch manufacturer sits at the cutting edge of watchmaking technology. Patek Philippe maintains a tradition of innovation, boasting an impressive repertoire of more than 80 patents, including the Minute Repeater, Annual Calendar, and the Perpetual Calendar.
